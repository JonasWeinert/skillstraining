# Responsible Language Guide

## Preferred Terms

- "flagged for review"
- "requires follow-up"
- "needs reconciliation"
- "possible data-entry or implementation issue"
- "descriptive change"

## Avoid

- "fraud" without confirmed evidence;
- "bad enumerator";
- "noncompliant household";
- "program impact" for before/after comparisons;
- blaming providers, households, or field staff from data flags alone.

## Required Caveats

Use a caveat whenever reporting:

- welfare changes;
- enumerator quality flags;
- payment discrepancies;
- open high-severity field visits;
- leakage or exclusion.
