# Example Claim Rewrites

| Risky wording | Better wording |
|---|---|
| The program caused school attendance to rise. | School attendance is higher at follow-up; this descriptive change is not a causal impact estimate. |
| Enumerator EN03 performed badly. | EN03 has an unusually high short-duration rate and should be reviewed by a supervisor. |
| MobileCash failed households. | MobileCash has the highest mean payment delay and requires operational follow-up. |
| These households lied about receipt. | Administrative and self-reported receipt do not match for these households and should be reconciled. |
