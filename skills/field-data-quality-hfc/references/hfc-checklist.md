# Lumora HFC Checklist

Grounded in `Lecture Slides - Data quality assurance.pdf`.

## Completeness

- Unique, non-missing IDs.
- Follow-up response against baseline frame.
- Submission counts by district, village, date, and enumerator.

## Distribution

- Implausible household sizes.
- Missing PMT values.
- Survey duration outliers.
- GPS accuracy outliers.
- High "don't know" and module missing counts.

## Consistency

- Baseline versus follow-up household fields.
- Self-reported transfer receipt versus payment records.
- Field visit district versus household district.
- Back-check discrepancies.

## Survey-Specific

- Enumerator short-duration rates.
- Refusal and consent patterns.
- Quality flags by enumerator and district.

## Operational Priority

Prioritize cases with:

- repeated failed payments;
- unresolved high-severity field visits;
- back-check follow-up required;
- admin/self-report receipt mismatch.
