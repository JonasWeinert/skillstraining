# Mock World Bank-Style Deck Guidance

This training project uses a mock institutional style, not official World Bank
brand assets.

Use:

- white backgrounds;
- World Bank blue accent: `#005EB8`;
- secondary cyan: `#00A3E0`;
- restrained orange for operational cautions: `#D55E00`;
- clean sans-serif typography;
- one main claim per slide;
- generated figures and tables as evidence.

Avoid:

- decorative clutter;
- unsupported causal language;
- copying numbers by hand;
- restricted or unofficial logos.
